FAITH APOSTOLIC LIGHTHOUSE ANNUAL CONVENTION

Be strong, and let your heart take courage, all you who wait for the LORD!

Psalm 31:24

In these trying times it can be hard to walk the righteous path and serve our Lord faithfully. We are here to be our brothers keeper, we will help you up if you falter. Join with worsheppers from over 20 countries as we lift our voices up to the Lord in Praise & Worship.

2021-11-19(THU) ~ 20(FRI)

@ Montego Bay Convention Centre

Main Program

  • Service

    Elevate your understanding of Gods word by joining our services where preachers share their insight.

  • Bible Study

    Come ask questions, discuss, and study Gods word with people who are willing to listen and build on your input.

  • Children Village

    Bring your kids to our interactive and fun children villages. Here we share bible stories in the form of plays and sing along songs.

  • Prayer Meeting

    Join us in our Prayer Meetings where we converse with our Lord and request his blessings guidance and protection.
